📖 HOW TO PLAY
❤️
THE PUZZLE

Each round shows a Heart pattern image. Find the missing number (0–9) and tap it!

🛡️
DEFEND

Press DEFEND and solve the puzzle to block incoming enemy damage completely.

⏱️
TIMER

Each puzzle has a time limit. Running out of time counts as a wrong answer!

💔
WRONG ANSWER

Incorrect answers reduce YOUR health. Think carefully before answering!

👹
ENEMY AI

The enemy counter-attacks each round. Defend correctly to avoid damage.

🏆
WIN CONDITION

Reduce enemy HP to 0. Don't let YOUR HP reach 0 first!
