⚔ Battle Basics
01
❤️
The Puzzle

Each round displays a Heart pattern image. Your job is to find the missing number (0–9) hidden in the pattern and tap it before time runs out.

02
⚔
Attack Mode

Default mode. Solve the puzzle correctly to deal damage to the enemy. The faster and more accurate you are, the more combos you build.

03
🛡️
Defend Mode

Press DEFEND before answering. Solve it correctly to completely block the enemy's next attack. A wrong answer while defending still hurts you!

04
⏱️
The Timer

Every puzzle has a countdown based on your chosen difficulty (10–30 seconds). If time runs out, it counts as a wrong answer and the enemy attacks!

05
💔
Wrong Answer

Incorrect answers reduce YOUR health. The enemy also counter-attacks every round regardless. Stay sharp — mistakes are costly!

06
🏆
Win Condition

Reduce the enemy's HP to 0 before your own HP hits 0. Earn coins after every battle based on your accuracy, combo, and HP remaining.

🏪 The Power-Up Shop

Earn 🪙 coins after each battle and spend them in the Forge Shop before battle begins. Power-ups are consumed when activated during combat — use them wisely!

🛡
IRON SHIELD
🪙 30 COINS
Grants a one-time full block. The next enemy attack that would damage you is completely absorbed — no HP loss at all.
USE WHEN: low HP · facing hard difficulty
🔥
BATTLE RAGE
🪙 40 COINS
Activates a rage buff that doubles your attack damage for the next correct answer. Perfect for finishing off a weakened enemy fast.
USE WHEN: enemy near 0 HP · combo active
💚
HEALING POTION
🪙 25 COINS
Instantly restores 5 HP mid-battle. Simple but crucial when you're clinging to survival. Works on any warrior including the Knight.
USE WHEN: HP is critically low · any time
❄️
FROST BIND
🪙 35 COINS
Freezes the enemy for one round, preventing their counter-attack entirely. Gives you a free damage turn with zero retaliation risk.
USE WHEN: attack mode · securing kill
💡 Pro Tips
⚔ FORGE WISDOM ⚔
🎯Use keyboard shortcuts — press 0–9 to answer, A to attack, D to defend. Much faster than clicking!
🔥Mage is best for aggressive players — land 3 correct answers in a row and your 4th hit deals 3× damage.
🛡On Hard difficulty, Knight + Iron Shield is the safest combo. 35 HP + 1 free block can save your life.
⚡Archer + Hard difficulty is a fun challenge — Swift Shot keeps adding time, so you'll never feel rushed.
🌑Ninja's dodge is completely passive — no input needed. Great for players who struggle with defend timing.
💀Berserker is high-risk, high-reward. Play aggressively, let your HP drop low, then unleash 2× damage to close out.
💰Coins are earned based on accuracy, max combo, and HP remaining — a perfect run earns far more than a sloppy win.
❄️Frost Bind + Mage combo is devastating — freeze the enemy, then land a 3-combo Arcane Surge for massive damage.
