📖 HOW TO PLAY
MASTER THE FORGE — DOMINATE THE ARENA
01
❤️
The Puzzle

Each round shows a Heart pattern image. Find the missing number (0–9) and tap it!

02
🛡️
Defend

Press DEFEND and solve the puzzle to block all incoming enemy damage completely.

03
⏱️
Timer

Each puzzle has a time limit. Running out of time counts as a wrong answer!

04
💔
Wrong Answer

Incorrect answers reduce YOUR health. Think carefully before answering!

05
👹
Enemy AI

The enemy counter-attacks each round. Defend correctly to avoid taking damage.

06
🏆
Win Condition

Reduce enemy HP to 0. Don't let YOUR HP reach 0 first — glory awaits!
